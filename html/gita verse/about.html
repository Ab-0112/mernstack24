तत्रैवं सति कर्तारमात्मानं केवलं तु यः।
पश्यत्यकृतबुद्धित्वान्न स पश्यति दुर्मतिः।।18.16।।

Now, such being the case, verily he who, owing to an untrained understanding, looks upon his Self, which is isolated, as the agent, he of perverted intelligence does not see.

GitaGyaan

GitaGyaan is a platform or concept centered around spreading the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita. It focuses on providing clear and accessible teachings from the Gita, often interpreted to align with modern life challenges and spiritual quests. Through GitaGyaan, individuals can explore the principles of Karma (selfless action), Bhakti (devotion), Jnana (knowledge), and Dhyana (meditation), offering guidance on living a balanced, purposeful, and spiritually enriched life. The platform aims to demystify the profound lessons of the Gita, making them applicable for people of all walks of life.
